What is Iraq Hackathon ?

Iraq Hackathon is planned to be 52 hours event, and it is founded and organized by Plus the Edge Company for Business and Technology consultancy. Iraq Hackathon will gather all the creative individuals all over Iraq and give them the opportunity to show up their ideas and get supports. It is where creative designers, coders, and web developers come together to bring an innovative idea to the country and solve a problem.

Iraq hackathon will be an online hackathon where Plus the Edge will use all of its resources to make this online hackathon happen. Plus the Edge owns the official license of Adobe Connect which is used to do virtual meeting, training, and webinars. Evaluation Criteria

All groups are expected to have an idea that solves a problem in the world using any kinds of technology, web applications, AI, and etc in three different fields, fintech, art and healthcare. By the end of 52 hours of working, participants must have a prototype of their work and they will be evaluated based on the below criteria.

  • 1. Theme: Natural Phenomena Disasters. Does the idea solve a natural phenomenon issue directly or indirectly ?

    3 Points

  • 2. Importance of the Idea. How significant is the solution to the problem that is solving?

    5 points

  • 3. Business Value. Does the idea bring value to a specific market? Or solve a specific problem in a market? Does it make money? Is the business model clear?

    5 points

  • 4. Realistic Capability. The three days aside, how realistic is the technology to be put into production?

    5 Points

  • 5. Innovation and Creativity. How is the solution different from the other existing technologies? Is there any elements of creativity in prototype created?

    5 points

FAQ

1If I need some items for my project, can you provide them?

No, you have to bring your own laptop or any other items like (microcontroller) that you think you will need them. Only electric extensions will be provided.

2Can I sleep during my three day stay?

You can take rest or sleep, however no accommodation is provided. If you would like to sleep, you can bring your sleeping bag or some couches are available at the venue that you can use.

3Do I have to pay for attending the event?

If you have an idea or you want to join a group to work on an idea, you should register as a participant, which is free of charge. While if you would like to attend the event as a guest to witness the event for opening and closing ceremony or full event days packages are available.

4How can I apply to be a volunteer?

Email us at career@csffco.com with the email subject “Hackathon Volunteering”

5I live outside of Japan, can I still apply?

Yes, you can. The event is international and everyone can attend.

6Do you pay for my airfare tickets and visa?

No, you have to cover the fees for both airplane tickets and visa.

7What is the language of the event?

English is the official language of the event.

8Do you provide accommodations?

we don't provide any accommodations. While the venue is open for the three days of the event and you can stay there during day and night time.
